About Internship & Job Fair

Finding the right talent as well as finding the best Internship or Job profile is difficult for both companies and applicants. Internship and Job Fair (IJF) brings a very innovative solution to this problem. It provides a platform for companies to hire India’s best talent for different profiles, and a platform for students to get an internship or a placement in these companies.
